Italian Soul, New York Spirit

Experience the level of culinary tradition at the heart of Ditmas Park, Brooklyn.

A Singular Approach

Located at the heart of Cortelyou Road, Garlic & Olive provides the perfect ambiance to savor the flavors of Italian fine dining. Our pasta is freshly made daily, and we serve only the finest local ingredients in every dish we serve.

Chef's Selections

Antipasto Skewers with Tortellini

Olive, cherry tomatoes, bocconcini mozzarella, and fresh basil.

Tagliatelle ai Funghi

Delicate tagliatelle pasta with sautéed mixed mushrooms in a creamy mascarpone sauce.

Shrimp Scampi

Sautéed shrimp in garlic olive oil, white wine, fresh lemon juice, and a touch of red pepper flakes.

The Bar

A sanctuary for the thirsty traveler. Our bar honors the classics while embracing the new. Featuring an extensive collection of wines, a curated list of Italian vintages, and local brews that define New York evenings.
